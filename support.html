Knight's Tour Support

Knight's Tour is a classic chess puzzle game where you guide a knight to visit every square on the board exactly once. Choose from 5 difficulty levels (4×4 to 8×8) and challenge yourself!

How to Play

  • Tap any square to start your knight's journey
  • Tap valid moves (highlighted squares) to continue
  • Complete the board by visiting all squares exactly once
  • Use hints when stuck (tap the hint button)
  • Undo moves if you need to backtrack

Features

  • 5 difficulty levels from beginner to expert
  • Track your stats: wins, completion percentage, average time
  • Sound effects (toggle on/off in settings)
  • Beautiful medieval-inspired design

Privacy

Knight's Tour collects anonymous gameplay analytics to improve the game experience. No personal information is collected or shared. Read our full Privacy Policy.
